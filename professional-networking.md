## Reflection Using STAR Technique

**Situation:**
As a student preparing to enter the technology industry, I recognized the importance of establishing a professional online presence and building connections within the ICT community.

**Task:**
My objective was to create a comprehensive LinkedIn profile that accurately represents my skills and academic achievements, while beginning to build a professional network that could support my career development.

**Action:**
I optimized my LinkedIn profile with a professional photo, detailed summary of my education and skills, and descriptions of relevant academic projects. I connected with classmates, lecturers, and professionals in the technology field. I also joined industry-related groups and began engaging with content relevant to my career interests.

**Result:**
My LinkedIn profile now serves as a dynamic professional portfolio that has helped me make valuable connections in the industry. I've received endorsements for my technical skills and have had informative exchanges with professionals who have provided career advice. This has expanded my understanding of career paths in ICT and opened doors to potential opportunities.
